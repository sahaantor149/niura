Ingenium, an award ceremony organized by NYIT College of Engineering and Computing Sciences, celebrates the achievements of alumni and industry partners. The event provides a networking platform for students who are interested. Ingenium emphasizes the college's commitment to the engagement of the community and global citizenship, mentioning contributions like 3D printing face shields for first responders to many more. The awards event furthers the college's mission to provide outstanding academic programs to foster faculty dedication for student centered learning by engaging in real world projects or nurturing partnerships with employers, alumni and the broader community. The awards presented are the “Hall of Fame”, “Rising Star”, and “Industry Partner of the Year”. Those who receive the Hall of Fame award are recognized as exemplary alumni that have accomplished great things in their profession field and exceptional service to their profession , community and college. The Rising Star award recognizes alumni who have a strong commitment serving as a speaker, mentor and resource to current students. Lastly, Industry Partner of the Year recognizes a company playing an important role in the colleges growt and transformation and provides service to the college and their students.

The Ingenium Annual Awards Gala, hosted by the NYIT College of Engineering & Computing Sciences, is a prestigious event that celebrates and honors outstanding achievements in the field of engineering and technology. The gala brings together students and professionals for recognition and inspiration. With a focus on innovation, leadership, and academic excellence, the Ingenium Awards Gala highlights the remarkable contributions of individuals and organizations that have made significant advancements in technology and engineering, fostering a culture of innovation and and driving the future of engineering and computing sciences forward.
