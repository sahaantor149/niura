NXTUS, a Wichita entrepreneurial organization, runs an annual community health and vibrancy competition in tandem with half a dozen Kansan partners and BlueCross. Now in its third year, the competition identifies a handful of startups out of a pool of over 100 to work with its partners on improving the health of Kansans. Each local partner awards select companies pilot projects–most competitors do not receive any offers while some are awarded pilot programs by more than one partner. The competition’s partners include a mix of Kansas NGOs (such as LiveWell Northwest Kansas) and county development boards from across the state, while competitors come from across the country. Participants are able to interact with local leaders to learn firsthand about the problems faced by Kansans so they are better able to improve their products moving forward.

At Kansas Blue, pilot projects and potential projects, aimed at tackling key issues such as access to mental health care and addiction treatment, improving senior care and expanding charitable giving in Kansas, are chosen for investment. The NXTSTAGE programs assist early-stage companies in effectively connecting with potential customers. Startups with a growth-oriented mindset who participate in NXTSTAGE accelerators or pilot competitions gain access to valuable resources, coaching, and the opportunity to engage with forward-thinking organizations in Kansas. There are 4 startup programs: NXTSTAGE Customer Traction Cohort provides customized coaching and workshops; NXTSTAGE Enterprise Engagement Series creates customer pathways between early-stage companies in Kansas and larger enterprises; NXTSTAGE Community Health & Vibrancy Pilot Competition makes Kansas communities with startups offering innovative tools to address community health; and NXTSTAGE Financial Technology Pilot Competition enables startups to present their technology tools to Kansas banks, credit unions and financial services companies.

NXTUS, a Wichita based startup, acts as a hub for business owners, corporate partners, and the community at large. By fostering the growth of startups and enhancing the innovation culture. They are also the proud presenters of the 2022 NXTSTAGE Community Health & Vibrancy Pilot Competition. The competition enables leading entrepreneurs to showcase their technological innovations to kansas organizations who work towards improving the health of the Kansas Community. These technological innovations are an attempt to address important concerns including senior care improvement, increasing philanthropic giving in Kansas, and provide access to mental health and addiction treatment. There are several startups who help these presenters take their idea to the next stage by providing exciting technology tools to bring their ideas to fruition.Akesa Health, Caregivers Insight and Debtle to mention a few.

We are excited to announce that our company, Niura, was featured as the semifinalists in the prestigious Kansas City Blue Cross competition on the Blue KC Healthcare Innovation Track. The University of Missouri-Kansas City's Henry W. Bloch School of Management and the famous Reigner Institute for Entrepreneurship & Innovation together hosted this competition, which gave up-and-coming businesspeople a stage to present their healthcare solutions. Being chosen as the semifinalist is an extraordinary accomplishment in the face of stiff competition from more than 90 participants from 12 prestigious colleges across Missouri, Kansas, Iowa and Nebraska.
